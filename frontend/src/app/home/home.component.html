Why We are?

In a world where compassion converges with purpose, our non-profit organization is dedicated to a pivotal mission: the elimination of hunger and the reduction of food wastage. At the core of our initiative is a synergistic collaboration between restaurants and NGOs. We adeptly retrieve surplus food from benevolent eateries, serving as a conduit that links surplus abundance to pressing needs. This surplus is transformed into sustenance, efficiently distributed by NGOs to those contending with hunger. Furthermore, individuals are encouraged to integrate their philanthropy into this fabric, contributing as their hearts lead them to the impactful NGOs. United by the force of collective benevolence, we are crafting a future where hunger is eradicated for everyone.

We are working with...

Restaurant's

The surplus food inventory at partnering restaurants is meticulously documented and highlighted on our website. Subsequently, dedicated volunteers from our partnered NGOs swiftly mobilize, reaching out to these establishments. They adeptly collect both cooked and raw surplus food, ensuring nothing goes to waste. This bounty is then distributed strategically within the areas covered by the NGOs, bringing nourishment to those in need. This streamlined system exemplifies the impactful synergy between compassionate restaurants, generous donors, and our committed NGO partners, creating a chain of kindness that fights hunger at its roots.

Non-Government organization (NGOs)

Our community/site operates collaboratively with NGOs to address food wastage and alleviate hunger. At the close of each day, participating restaurants input data on surplus food onto our platform. This information is made accessible to registered NGOs, empowering them to identify and collect surplus food from the restaurants. This streamlined process facilitates a more efficient and targeted distribution system, ensuring that excess food is redirected to those in need. By bridging the gap between food providers and NGOs, our platform serves as a catalyst for reducing food waste and making a meaningful impact on the lives of the less fortunate in our community.

Donators

Donating on our site is a straightforward process designed to connect generous individuals with those in need. When a donor wishes to contribute, they can browse through a list of participating restaurants on our platform. Once a restaurant is selected, donors can choose specific menu items they wish to donate and specify the quantity. Upon confirmation, the chosen restaurant receives a notification to prepare the designated food for donation. Subsequently, our partnered NGO is alerted and arranges to collect the donated items. This seamless system ensures that the generous contributions of individuals quickly translate into tangible assistance for the less fortunate, creating a direct and efficient channel for charitable giving through our platform.
